Who Is CIS?

CIS Security was founded in 2022 by determined student at dunwoody tech, Grant Cooper. The goal was to create a security company with top level services, that anyone and any company could partner with to secure their data and build a network impervious to todays cyber threats.

Founder of CIS Security - Grant Cooper

Grant Cooper founded CIS Security in 2022 while attending Dunwoody College of Technology. His mission was to start a security company that would allow large businesses, and every day people to have the same top level security.

About

CIS Security is a small security firm dedicated to giving top notch security to both moderate size organizations, and every day tech users. We strive to make every organization and every day user as safe as possible while they conduct busines, or do their every day web browsing.
